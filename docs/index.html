Why teams use it

Built for imperfect CRM exports, not just exact matches.

Cluster-first output

The engine connects duplicate pairs into merge-ready clusters so reviewers can act on a group instead of manually reconciling disconnected matches.

Explainable scoring

Matching reasons travel with each recommendation, making it obvious whether a pair matched on email, phone, normalized company name, domain, or location context.

Practical master selection

Master records are chosen by completeness so merge reviews start from the strongest surviving record instead of an arbitrary first match.

System flow

Three steps from CSV export to merge plan.

01

Normalize records

Clean email, phone, person-name, company-name, domain, and website signals into comparable values.

02

Score candidate pairs

Use object-specific rules to compare likely pairs and keep only matches above the configured threshold.

03

Build merge clusters

Group connected duplicates, choose the most complete master record, and emit review-friendly output.
